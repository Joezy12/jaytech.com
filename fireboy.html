Fireboy Dml

REAL NAME: Adedamola Adefolahan

Birth

    Adedamola Adefolahan was born into a Christian home and was raised in the same faith. Beyond the good morals that his parents confidence instilled in him, it also created a love for music in him. Because he and his parents attended church regularly, he quickly developed a soft spot for the church's choir and became deeply fascinated with the process involved in making music. He soon got his chance to test the waters of creative gift when he became older and joined the choir. During this period, the flame of artistry in Fireboy DML was born.

EDUCATION

    Fireboy DML grew up in Abeokuta, Ogun state where he attended both his primary and secondary school education there. To further his education, he went to the prestigious Obafemi Awolowo University (OAU) in Ile-Ife, Osun State. At OAU, Fireboy DML studied English and graduated in 2017 after earning a Bachelor in Arts (BA).

    Career

    Fireboy has acknowledged that the love of arts grew on him, as he is not from a family of artists. While he has written poems for most of his adult life, the artist knew that he could sing since he was 12 years old. He always considered singing something he could do but not as a serious talent and not something he could even make money from. While he always wrote poems and some song lyrics, it was at the university where he embraced his talent.
    He began spending more time writing songs, which he recorded in a studio around the school's campus. With time, he became part of the institution's emerging artists, including the likes of rappers Chinko Ekun, Cheque, Blaqbonez, and singer, Asake.
    Fireboy DML's success as an artist was sealed when he became acquainted with YBNL Records' boss Olamide. The older artiste, who was impressed by his sound and songwriting ability, decided to sign him under YBNL Records.
