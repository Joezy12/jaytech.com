About Us

SOLAR ENERGY And Power Transformers Nig. Ltd -SEAPS is totally committed to providing technological advancement and environment sensitive power back-up solutions to the dynamic needs of people, all over the nation and internationally.

SEAPS uses the Photovoltaic Technology, as a step to conversion of solar light into electricity, and over the years it has proved to be one of the most powerful instruments in bringing electricity to even the most remote regions of the world.

SEAPS is registered under the Federal Republic of Nigeria, Companies and Allied Matters, Decree 1990, and the objectives for which the company is established are: to carry on the business of renewable energy systems and components, solar modules (panels) manufacture, kits, controllers, inverters, general D.C. and A.C. supply, seminars, installation, exhibitions, importers, exporters, manufacturer’s representative, general merchants, solar electricity in all its ramifications.

TO DESIGN: supply and install Solar Powered Systems, industrial merchandising and marketing of Power Protection Equipments.

TO CARRY: on the business of solar arrays, solar power wind energy, research facilities, energy storage and photovoltaic, solar batteries, tabular plates, single cell, vented lead-acid batteries, modular systems.

TO CARRY: on the business of solar powered systems, inverters back-up systems, charge controllers, satellite telecommunication, radio, telephone, fax, computer, printers, telecommunications systems network for unmanned station and maintenance.

TO CARRY: on in Nigeria or elsewhere in the world, business as the designers of, distributors of, repairs of, buyers, sellers, distributors of suppliers of the dealer (whether as wholesalers of retailers) in all kind of electrical, electronic, mechanical, equipment’s, instruments, components, spares, accessories, appliances or other materials which are related directly or indirectly and to carry out all such operations and transactions as are incidental ditto.

TO ALMAGAMATE: with any other company whose objectives are or include objective similar to those of this company, whether by sale or purchase (for fully or partly paid-up share or otherwise) of the undertaken, subject to the liability of this or any such other company as aforesaid, with or without winding up or sale purchase (for fully or partly paid up shares or otherwise) of all, or a controlling interest in, the shares or stock of this any other company as aforesaid, or by, partnership, or in any other manner.
